ZA'ATAR RECIPE

A fragrant Middle Eastern mix that will instantly add a savoury spice to any dish

Alejandro Lopez

Recipe for one jar

PREP

7 mins

COOK

2 mins

TOTAL

9 mins

  • 2 tbsp cumin

  • 2 tbsp sesame seeds

  • ½ 2 tbsp dried oregano

  • 2 tbsp sumac

[INTRO - Lightly toast the cumin seeds in a dry pan, just until they start to release their aroma.]

  • Grind them to a fine powder using a pestle and mortar then stir in all the other ingredients plus 2 tsp salt and 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper

  • It is now ready to use in your recipes.

  • Keep in a clean jar or airtight container for a month..

  • Cool and refrigerate if necessary (2 to 3 days). A little goes a long way.
