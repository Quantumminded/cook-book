SHAWARMA SPICE BLEND

SHAWARMA SPICE BLEND

Like the store bought shawarma seasoning but way better!

Alina Nenhot

Recipe for a jar

PREP

5 mins

TOTAL

5 mins

  • ¼ cup ground black pepper

  • ¼ cup ground allspice

  • ½ ¼ cup garlic powder

  • 2 tablespoons ground cloves

  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

  • 2 tablespoons ground nutmeg

  • 2 tablespoons ground cardamom

  • 1 tablespoon chili powder

  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano

  • 1 tablespoon salt

[INTRO - Combine all the ingredients in a bowl or ziplock bag. Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.]

  • To marinate the shawarma meat: you'll need 2 tablespoons olive oil + 1 tablespoon white vinegar + ½ teaspoon salt.

  • Allow chicken to marinate for 10-20 minutes.

  • 1 hour to 24 hours for beef.

  • Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place.
