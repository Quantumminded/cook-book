HUNAN SAUCE

HUNAN SAUCE

A blend of sweet and sour with hot and spicy

HUNAN SAUCE

Julie Awad

Sauce for one stir-fry recipe

PREP

30 mins

COOK

30 mins

TOTAL

60 mins

  • 1 tb Bean paste hot, or chilli

  • 1tb Fresh ginger root

  • ½ c Soy Sauce

  • 1tb Garlic minced

[INTRO - Hunan or Honan is a style of cooking that blend sweet and sour with hot and spicy. Hot and Sour soup is a Hunan dish. The sauce]

  • Prepare the hot and sweet sour mixtures in separate bowls.

  • Heat 2 tablespoon of oil Stir fy the "HOT" mix until fragrant.

  • Add the "Sweet and Sour" blend and cook until liquid thickens.

  • Cool and refrigerate if necessary (2 to 3 days). A little goes a long way.
